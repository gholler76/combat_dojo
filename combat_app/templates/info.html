WELCOME TO COMBAT DOJO!

"A retro turn-based combat game"

About the Game:

Fighters are rated on a scale of 1-20 in combat attributes:

  • PWR (Power) - The level of damage an attack a fighter can inflict.
  • ATT (Attack) - The overall quality of a fighter's attack ability.
  • DEF (Defense) - The overall quality of a fighter's defense ability.
  • SPD (Speed) - Factors into a fighter's ability to attack and defend quickly.
  • AGL (Agility) - The fighter's ability to avoid attacks.
  • RCV (Recovery) - The fighter's ability to regain Health throughout the fight.

Fighters take turns attacking and defending. The game ends when a fighter has 0 Health, or whoever has the most health after 40 rounds.

Gameplay:

Each round, you will either ATTACK or DEFEND by selecting a technique and pressing the GO button.

  • *** ATTACK TECHNIQUES ***
    • QUICK - Less damage but higher chance of success.
    • NORMAL - Average damage and success rate.
    • STRONG - Higher damage but less chance of success.
  • *** DEFENSE TECHNIQUES ***
    • DODGE - Evade an attack with less than average Health recovery.
    • BLOCK - Block an attack with average Health recovery.
    • COUNTER - Counter an attack with minimal Health recovery while also inflicting damage to the attacker.
